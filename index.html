Art-Level Custom Sneakers

Wild out your alphet with tailored kicks | Designed, transformed, and hand-painted by skillful sneaker-heads.

Passion for art. Obsession for shooz.

Our mission is simple - Get you the greatest sneakers of all times. No Maypops, no beaters, no monkey plastic. We want you to wear a piece of art, a poem of leather and laces. That's why we paired our insane love for sneakers with our heavy-duty creative talent to create you a pair of kicks so unique, so creatively designed, so well crafted, such a representation of your style and soul that they crack the floor on every step you take - if you ever get to wear them at all.

You ain't gonna buy this kicks.
You commission them. That's art.

Highs, mids, and lows. Jayz, Loubs, and Chucks. Superheros, patterns, or classical art. We can do a wide range of serious pimps to any style of kicks, whether you bring your own white-cokes or pick one from our canvas catalog. Each project is highly unique, so are the prices. But regardless of your crazy ideas, we will mostly charge based on:

The Canvas

This is the type of kicks you want to customize. Things like its material, structure, size, and current condition (if it's not Deadstock). All that influences the design and the work needed.

The Design

This refers to all the work we put into empathizing with your vision and ideas and working upon all that to make it a dope design, not only aesthetic but also functional and practical.

The Art Work

Here we estimate on the amount of time, work, skills, materials, and any other add-ons we will put into your kicks to turn them into royal jewelry that could arguably be worn on kings' heads.
